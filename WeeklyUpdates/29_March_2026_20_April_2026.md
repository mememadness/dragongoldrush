## Highlights: Dragon Token Economy Pivot

A major strategic shift this period was moving away from UTXOSwap as the primary payment mechanism and building a native Dragon Token credit system directly into the game.

**Previously**, players paid to play via UTXOSwap — a third-party DEX on Nervos CKB. However, UTXOSwap is no longer being actively maintained and the testnet version does not work and is unsupported, making it unviable as a core payment dependency. This forced a pivot to building our own in-game swap and credit system — the bulk of development work this period.

**Now**, Dragon Token is a first-class in-game currency:
- **Credits system:** Players purchase credit packs with Dragon tokens via a streamlined reserve-confirm flow, eliminating the need for per-game swaps
- **ACP cell architecture:** Anyone-Can-Pay cells on CKB allow direct Dragon token deposits with faster credit availability
- **Prize pool integration:** A portion of every credit purchase and spend flows into the monthly leaderboard prize pool, paid out in Dragon
- **Backend-authoritative inventory:** Special items are now server-validated and tied to the credit economy, preventing client-side manipulation
- **Dragon economics engine:** Built-in pricing, LP tracking, and admin dashboard integration — no longer dependent on external DEX availability

With UTXOSwap's testnet unsupported, we also had to stand up a **pre-prod environment running on mainnet** so we could test the full credit purchase and Dragon token flow with real assets. This was a necessary step to validate the ACP cell architecture and payment flows before going live.

UTXOSwap remains as a historical price fallback for Dragon/CKB conversion rates, but the game no longer routes player payments through it.
